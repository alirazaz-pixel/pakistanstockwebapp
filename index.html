About This Data

The KSE-100 level shown here is sourced from a public market-data aggregator (Yahoo Finance, ticker ^KSE), refreshed automatically every few minutes. It is provided for personal, informational tracking only and is not an official PSX feed. For licensed, official real-time PSX data, contact PSX's Market Data Team.

This app polls its local data file every 10 seconds so updates appear instantly once new data lands — the underlying values themselves refresh roughly every 5 minutes via an automated job.

Inflation Snapshot

CPI and core inflation are published monthly by the Pakistan Bureau of Statistics (PBS) and updated here by hand when new releases land — they don't change minute to minute like market prices.
